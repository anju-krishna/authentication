WELCOME TO GALAXY INTERNATIONALS

OUR HISTORY: In his endeavour to reduce leakage of refrigerant gas-Mafron, which depletes the ozone layer, Mr. Virender Kumar Aggarwal came up with the idea of starting his own refrigeration tool business. With the help of friends and guidance from industry experts, he successfully developed good quality charging lines and established Galaxy International in the year 1986. Within a span of 5-6 years, they came up with a new product - Junior Tube Cutter, to meet the market demand. With time, Galaxy International became a national brand. Since then, they have continued to add new products to their range as per the needs of the market.
